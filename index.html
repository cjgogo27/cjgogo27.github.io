About Me

I am a Master's student at Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU). Currently, I am applying for a Ph.D. program, focusing on the intersection of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

I am actively seeking Ph.D. opportunities starting Fall 2026/2027. I have a strong foundation in predictive models and machine learning, and my latest research tackles fundamental transportation routing and trajectories methodologies. If my profile aligns with your lab's interests, please feel free to reach out via email.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Transportation Modelling:

  • AI for Intelligent Transportation Systems: Developing advanced deep learning algorithms to model and predict complex routing and transport operations.
  • Trajectory Flow and Reinforcement Learning: Applying generative AI and robust predictive frameworks to analyze large-scale trajectories.
  • Interpretable Machine Learning: Focused on building reliable, transparent machine learning models to deploy in safety-critical transportation environments.
